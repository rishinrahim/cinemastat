Data and Methodology

This research is mostly based on the data scrapped from following two sources: MalayalaChalachithram.com, an internet cooperative for sharing comprehensive details of Malayalam Movies, Movie Songs and Movie Artists. and Wikipedia Malayalam movie Datasets, the free encyclopedia owned and distributed by wikipedia.org. All data, related to early years was ignored for both datasets.
